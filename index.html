Who We Are

A School Rooted in Tradition, Ready for Tomorrow

Founded on the timeless principles of the Gurukul system, Balya Vardhan Gurukul fosters curiosity, character, and creativity. Our school empowers children to excel academically while growing into compassionate, responsible citizens.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Every corner of our campus is designed to inspire learning, creativity, and well-being.

📚
Well-Stocked Library
Over 5,000 books including Hindi & English fiction, non-fiction, encyclopaedias, and competitive exam guides. Open daily for students and teachers.
🔬
Science Laboratory
Fully-equipped lab for Classes 6–8 with chemistry apparatus, biological specimens, and physics equipment for hands-on experimentation.
💻
Computer Lab
20-station lab with internet access and educational software. Students from Class 3 onwards get regular computer literacy sessions.
🏃
Spacious Playground
Large outdoor sports ground with cricket pitch, football goalposts, badminton court, and a dedicated play zone for junior classes.
🎨
Art & Craft Room
Dedicated studio for painting, clay modelling, origami, and craft. Student artwork is proudly displayed throughout the campus.
🎵
Music Room
Soundproofed music room with harmoniums, tablas, keyboards, and a vocal practice area. Home to our award-winning school choir.
🚌
Safe Transport
GPS-tracked school buses covering major routes in Ghaziabad. Female escorts accompany buses for junior classes. Safety is our priority.
🍱
Hygienic Canteen
Clean, affordable, and nutritious mid-day meals. Our in-house kitchen follows FSSAI standards and serves fresh home-style food daily.
🏥
Medical Room
First-aid room staffed with a trained nurse. Annual health check-ups, eye tests, and dental camps are conducted for all students.

Co-Curricular Activities

Learning at BVG is never confined to textbooks. Our rich programme of activities develops the whole child.

🎭
Annual Cultural Fest
A grand stage event each December featuring drama, dance, music, and fashion — every student participates.
🏆
Sports Day
Inter-class athletics, track events, kabaddi and team sports every January. Winners represent at district level.
🧪
Science Exhibition
Annual showcase of student-built models and experiments. Jury includes local scientists and engineers.
📣
Debate & Elocution
Weekly debates and speech competitions from Class 4 onwards, building confidence and critical thinking.

Art Competitions
Inter-school drawing, rangoli, and poster-making competitions held throughout the academic year.
📚
Reading Club
Book discussions, storytelling sessions, and a monthly "Best Reader" award to cultivate a lifelong reading habit.
🌿
Eco Club
Garden maintenance, tree plantation drives, and environment awareness campaigns led by students.
🧘
Yoga & Meditation
Daily 20-minute morning yoga and breathing exercises for all classes — body, mind, and balance.

Admissions 2025–26

Securing your child's future starts here. Follow these simple steps to join the BVG family.

1
Fill the Enquiry Form
Submit the enquiry form on this page or visit our school office between 9 AM – 2 PM on working days.
2
Receive Prospectus & Fee Details
Our admissions team will call you within 48 hours and share the prospectus, fee structure, and available seats.
3
School Visit & Interaction
Visit the campus for a tour and an informal interaction session with the class teacher and principal.
4
Document Submission & Fee Payment
Submit required documents (birth certificate, Aadhaar, previous marksheet) and pay the registration fee to confirm admission.
5
Welcome to BVG!
Your child receives their uniform kit, books list, and student ID. Classes begin as per the academic calendar.
